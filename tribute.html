"Sometimes life is going to hit you in the head with a brick. Don't lose faith."

Brief Timeline of Steve Jobs

  • 1955 - Born on 24 February in San Francisco, California
  • 1970 - Meets Steve Wozniak and became friends
  • 1972 - Graduates from Homestead High School, California and enrols at Reed College
  • 1976 - Co-founds Apple with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne
  • 1980 - Apple goes public, thus making Steve Jobs worth $200 million net worth
  • 1985 - Resigns as CEO of Apple and starts new venture called Next Computer
  • 1986 - Buys Pixar Animation Studios
  • 1995 - Becomes CEO and President of Pixar Studios
  • 1996 - Apple buys Next and returns as an adviser
  • 1997 - Becomes interim CEO of Apple
  • 1998 - Apple becomes profitable and introduces the iMac
  • 2000 - Drops the interim title and becomes CEO of Apple
  • 2001 - Introduces Apple Retail Stores and iPods, which both would go on to be successful
  • 2004 - Had a surgery to remove a cancerous tumour in his pancreas
  • 2006 - Disney buys Pixar and Steve Jobs joins the board of Disney as top shareholder
  • 2007 - Introduces the iPhone and inducted in the California Hall of Fame
  • 2009 - Takes a 6-month medical leave
  • 2011 - In August Steve Jobs resigns as CEO of Apple and becomes Chairman
  • 2011 - Passed away on 5 October
